The UCSB Aquatic Invertebrate Lab is part of CCBER (Cheadle Center for Biodiversity and Ecological Restoration). CCBER fulfills the university's mission of education, research, and outreach through its stewardship and restoration of campus lands and preservation and management of natural history collections.
